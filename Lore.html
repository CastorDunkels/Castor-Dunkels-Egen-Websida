Story

long before the game takes place there was an ancient race of humanoid beings called pthumerians who lived together with godlike beings called great ones deep underground. Sometime most of the great ones ascended from the mortal plane which left the pthumerians with their corpses. The pthumerians used the blood of the great ones as a healing substance. Over time the pthumerians became overrun by a scourge of beasts leading to them dying. Much later the humans discoverd these underground tombs and explored them.
